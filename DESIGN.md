# Velo Design System (v2.0 - Jordan Redesign)

This design system is optimized for the Jordan taxi market, focusing on trust, reliability, and high-end aesthetics.

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Velo
- **Vibe**: Premium, Reliable, Modern, Sleek.
- **Audience**: Jordan-based commuters and travelers.

## Color Palette (Slate & Rose)
- **Primary/Accent**: `#E11D48` (Velo Rose - used for CTAs and highlights)
- **Surface**: `#F8FAFC` (Soft Slate White)
- **Neutral 900**: `#0F172A` (Deep Slate - Headlines & Icons)
- **Neutral 700**: `#334155` (Mid Slate - Body text)
- **Success**: `#10B981` (Emerald)
- **Warning**: `#F59E0B` (Amber)

## Typography (Arabic First)
- **Fonts**: Inter (English), Outfit (English), Arabic font of choice (IBM Plex Sans Arabic recommended).
- **H1**: 32pt, Bold, -0.02em tracking.
- **Body**: 16pt, Regular, 1.5 line-height.
- **Labels**: 12pt, Semi-bold, Uppercase for English.

## Spacing & Shape
- **Base Unit**: 4px
- **Corner Radius**: 16px (Standard), 24px (Large Containers)
- **Elevation**: Soft shadows (0px 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.05))

## Core Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded (14px), No elevation, Vibrant Rose background with White text.
- **Input Fields**: Slate-100 background, 1.5pt border on focus.
- **Cards**: Surface color, 1pt Slate-200 border, soft shadow.

## Jordan UX Rules
1. **RTL Priority**: All layouts must be mirrorable for Arabic (Right-to-Left).
2. **Landmark Pickups**: UI should prioritize well-known landmarks over raw GPS addresses.
3. **Price Transparency**: Always show estimate chips (Surge, Tolls, Airport) before booking.
4. **Trust Signals**: Driver identity cards must be prominent with rating and "Certified" badges.
